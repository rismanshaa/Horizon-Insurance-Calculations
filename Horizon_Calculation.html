1. Premi Netto (Equivalence Principle)

P = APV(Benefits) / äxy:n| dimana: APV(Benefits) = APV(Death Benefit) + APV(Joint Annuity Retirement) äxy:n| = Σ(t=0 to n-1) vᵗ · tpxy^(Clayton) v = 1/(1+i) [discount factor]
Prinsip ekuivalensi: EPV premi = EPV manfaat pada t=0 (MLC §7.2)

2. Joint Life Survival — Clayton Copula

C(u,v;θ) = [u^(-θ) + v^(-θ) - 1]^(-1/θ) [Clayton Copula] tpxy^(Clayton) = C(tpx, tpy; θ) = [(tpx)^(-θ) + (tpy)^(-θ) - 1]^(-1/θ) Independent: tpxy = tpx · tpy Dependent: tpxy = C(tpx, tpy; θ=2) ≥ tpx · tpy [positive dependence] tqxy = 1 - tpxy [joint life mortality] tpx̄y = tpx + tpy - tpxy [last survivor survival]
θ=2 mengacu Frees, Carriere & Valdez (1996): broken-heart syndrome effect pada pasangan.

3. APV Death Benefit — Fase Akumulasi

APV(DB) = Σ(t=0 to n-1) vᵗ⁺¹ · (t|qxy) · b(t+1) b(t+1) = ROP(t+1) jika t < 10 [Return of Premium — lihat definisi di bawah] b(t+1) = 50% × UP jika 10 ≤ t < 15 b(t+1) = 100% × UP jika t ≥ 15 t|qxy = tpxy - (t+1)pxy [curtate joint-life mortality] ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────── RETURN OF PREMIUM (sesuai draft produk): ROP(t) = Σ(s=0 to t) (Pg − Es) · (1+j)^(t−s) dimana: Pg = premi bruto yang dibayar tiap tahun Es = biaya yang berlaku pada tahun s (tahun 1: e₁ = 40%·Pg + fee_tahunan) (tahun 2+: eₛ = 8%·Pg + fee_tahunan) j = 4.5% (bunga teknis akumulasi premi) t−s = jarak waktu akumulasi (compounding) ROP(t) ≈ P_net · s̈_{t+1| 4.5%} [net premium proxy] = P_net · [(1.045^(t+1)−1)/0.045] · 1.045 Catatan: Pg − Es ≈ P_net (premi netto) karena expense loading dikembalikan ke pemegang polis berbasis premi netto. ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

4. Joint Life Annuity — Fase Distribusi (dengan Guaranteed Period)

Struktur anuitas 20-tahun term-due, mulai usia pensiun 60: ─── KOMPONEN 1: Kedua tertanggung hidup (100% anuitas) ─── APV_joint = A · Σ(k=0 to 19) vᵏ · kpxy(60) kpxy(60) = Clayton(kpx(60), kpy(60); θ) ─── KOMPONEN 2: Survivor (50% anuitas ke yang masih hidup) ─── APV_surv = 0.5·A · Σ(k=0 to 19) vᵏ · [kpx(60) − kpxy(60)] [X hidup, Y mati] + 0.5·A · Σ(k=0 to 19) vᵏ · [kpy(60) − kpxy(60)] [Y hidup, X mati] ─── KOMPONEN 3: Guaranteed Period 10 tahun ───────────────── Jika keduanya mati sebelum k=10, ahli waris terima 50% sisa s.d. thn ke-10. ä_certain:10| = Σ(k=0 to 9) vᵏ [pasti dibayar] ä_joint:10| = Σ(k=0 to 9) vᵏ · kpxy(60) [porsi sudah di Komp.1] APV_guarantee = 0.5·A · (ä_certain:10| − ä_joint:10|) = 0.5·A · Σ(k=0 to 9) vᵏ · [1 − kpxy(60)] ─── TOTAL APV ANUITAS (diukur dari t=0) ──────────────────── APV(Annuity) = npxy · vⁿ · [APV_joint + APV_surv + APV_guarantee] + APV(FTD lump sum) = npxy·vⁿ · Σk v^(k+1) · k|qxy · 0.5·UP dimana A = annualAnnuity = UP × 8%, n = tahun akumulasi

5. Triple Decrement Model

Decrements: (1) Mortality, (2) Lapse, (3) Surrender UDD in each decrement: q'x^(j) = qx^(j) · [1 - 0.5·qx^(k1)] · [1 - 0.5·qx^(k2)] k≠j Associated single decrements: q'x^(1) = qx [from TMI 2019 × mortality load] q'x^(2) = lapse_rate[t] [from product table] q'x^(3) = surrender_rate[t] [derived: 10% of q'(2)] tpx^(τ) = Π(s=0 to t-1) [1 - q'x+s^(1) - q'x+s^(2) - q'x+s^(3)] Active at t: lx^(τ) = l0 · tpx^(τ) [l0 = 100,000]

6. Prospective Reserve — Joint Life

DEFINISI (MLC §21 — Prospective Method): tV = APV_t(future benefits) − P_net · äxy+t:n-t| dimana: APV_t(future benefits) = EPV benefit yang BELUM dibayar, diukur pada t P_net · äxy+t:n-t| = EPV premi NETTO yang belum diterima äxy+t:n-t| = Σ(k=0 to n-t-1) vᵏ · kpxy+t^(Clayton) CATATAN PENTING: ✓ Reserve menggunakan P_NET (bukan P_gross) ✓ Tidak ada tambahan profit margin di sini — profit sudah termasuk dalam selisih P_gross vs P_net (expense & profit loading) ✗ tV ≠ APV_benefit − P_gross·ä (ini SALAH — profit dihitung dua kali) ✗ tV ≠ APV_benefit − P_net·ä + 15% (ini SALAH — +% bukan reserve) Rekursi Fackler (verifikasi): (tV + P_net) · (1+i) = q'xy+t^(mort) · b(t+1) + p'xy+t · (t+1)V + q'(lapse) · 0 + q'(surr) · SV(t) Non-negativity requirement: tV ≥ 0 ∀t (IFRS 17 / PSAK 74)

7. Gross Premium with Loading

P_gross = (P_net + Per-Policy Fee) / (1 - expense_ratio - profit_load) expense_ratio = 40% (year 1) → 8% (year 2+) → amortized as level loading Level Expense Loading (π_e): π_e = [e1 · äx + (e2-e1) · 1] / äxy:n| e1 = 40% × P_gross + 75,000/bln e2 = 8% × P_gross + 75,000/bln Profit Loading: π_θ = profit_pct × P_gross [10%/15%/22% per tier UP] P_gross = P_net + π_e + π_θ [solved iteratively]
